========================================
FIRESTORE DATABASE SETUP
========================================

Direct URL:
https://console.firebase.google.com/project/uniscrapper-pro/firestore

Steps:
1. Click the URL above
2. Click "Create database" button
3. Choose "Start in production mode"
4. Select a location (e.g., us-central or closest to you)
5. Click "Enable"
6. Wait 2-3 minutes for database to be ready

After database is created, you can deploy the backend!
